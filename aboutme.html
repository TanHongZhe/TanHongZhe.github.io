About Me



Hi! I'm Tan Hong Zhe and I am an enthusiastic student looking to pursue a career
in electrical engineering. Mathematics and physics have always fascinated
me, and I believe that they form the fundamental building blocks of this field.
Through this website, I hope to document my journey of exploring the intersection
between mathematics, physics, and electrical engineering. By delving into these
subjects and sharing my experiences, I aim to inspire and engage with like-minded
individuals who share a passion for these disciplines. Together, we can push the
boundaries of knowledge and contribute to the advancement of science and
technology. This website is inspired by the personal website of Tristan Chaang, a
renowned mathematician and prodigious talent
